Our work on objects
Large/Heavy Dry Objects

These are dry objects that require 2 people or more to move, and are predominantly in the cultural collection. Some objects may have labels attached to them.

These objects takes a large amount of space and need for special handling/ equipment.

We will use the Mobile 3D photogrammetry rig - portable framework with multiple high resolution cameras and professional lighting systems to digitise Large/Heavy Dry Objects.

It’ll take approximately 12 months and about $2,020,000 AUD to fully digitise all Large/Heavy Dry Objects.
